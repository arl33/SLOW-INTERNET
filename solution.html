List of Solutions

Root Causes:

  • Ethernet Cable Issues: Damaged or improperly connected cables can cause connectivity loss.
  • Poor Wi-Fi Signal Strength: Weak signals from distance or obstructions cause slowness and drops.
  • Outdated Infrastructure: Old equipment creates bottlenecks and security risks.

Best Solutions:

  • Report Issues: Notify IT or service providers for professional diagnosis and fixes.
  • Upgrade your router: Replace old routers for better range, speed, and efficiency.
  • Update network driver: Keep your device drivers updated for compatibility and performance.

Impact of Solutions:

  • Improved Productivity: Stable networks allow uninterrupted work.
  • Reduced Frustration: Fewer issues improve user experience.
  • Reliable Operations: Well-maintained infrastructure ensures consistent performance.
